Noli Me Tanghere Characters

Maria Clara
    A Girl who is very pretty/wealthy does not value material things, she was raised by being the daughter of Captain tiago but actually her real father is father damaso, Maria clara was inspired by leonor rivera and she is jose rizal's real-life childhood sweetheart.

      Crisostomo Ibarra
        is the novel's protagonist. The mestizo son of filipino business man Don Rafael Ibarra, he studied in europe for seven years. Ibarra is also Maria Clara's Fiance

        Padre Damaso

          Padre damaso was a priest who had raped maria clara's mother and then later on it turns out that damaso was the father of maria clara. He tries to prevent ibarra and maria clara getting married for her own sake.

          Don. Rafael ibarra

            Don. Rafael ibarra is the father of cristomo ibarra He was the wealthiest business man in San Diego He was an honest man and was well-respected by everyone.

            Elias

              Elias was a mysterious character who despite his deep religious beliefs resents both the spanish colonial government and the catholic church in the novel
